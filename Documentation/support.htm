Finding a solution

Use the SEARCH feature to search for keywords that describe the problem you are having. Chances are that other members have had the same problem at some point and your search will bring up many threads/posts that will have a solution.

How to Forum

http://forums.xmbforum.com/forumdisplay.php?fid=176

There are many good "How Tos" on different aspects of troubleshooting, running and enjoying your forums.

If all else fails...

Post your question, with as much detail as you can, in the support forums (see above for urls to the support forums) so that our Support Team can assist you.
