There is a problem with the way message links are stored and displayed in the u2u module. 
This is a fix that corrects such: 

Open u2u.php
 
just before: 

code:--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

eval("\$messages .= \"".template("u2u_row")."\";"); 

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
add:
code:--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

$message['msgfrom_enc'] = urlencode($message['msgfrom']);

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------


then, in the templates, (u2u_row), in the link to the profile, 
replace $message[msgfrom] in the URL to $message[msgfrom_enc], 
and leave the other one like it was
