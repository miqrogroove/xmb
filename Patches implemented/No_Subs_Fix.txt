[FIX] "No Subscriptions" message not showing

This will fix it so that when you do not have any subscriptions; 
and you try to view your list of subscriptions; it will display the proper message. 
Instead of just being blank. 


Open up your memcp_subscriptions_none template. 


FIND: 

$lang_nosubmsg 


REPLACE WITH: 

$lang_nosubsmsg 


Then save the changes.
